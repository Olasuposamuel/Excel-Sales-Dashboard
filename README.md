# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis (Excel Dashboard)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce sales dataset to identify **high value customer segments, top performing sales channels, and revenue driving products**.  

The goal is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights that support **data driven decision making in marketing, product strategy, and operations**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
E-commerce businesses often struggle to:
- Identify their most valuable customers  
- Understand which sales channels drive the most revenue  
- Determine which products contribute the highest sales  

This project addresses these challenges by providing a **clear, interactive Excel dashboard** that highlights key performance drivers.

---

## 🔑 Key Takeaways
- **Women aged 30–49** are the most valuable customer segment, contributing the highest share of orders and purchases  
- **Amazon** is the leading sales channel, generating the largest portion of revenue  
- The **“Set” product category** is the top-performing category, contributing nearly half of total sales  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 👥 Customer Demographics
- Women account for **64.05% of total purchases**  
- They also contribute **69.42% of total orders**, indicating higher engagement  
- The **30–49 age group** represents **34.59% of total orders**, making them a critical target segment  

### 📦 Order Fulfillment
- **92.25% of orders were successfully delivered**, reflecting strong operational efficiency  

### 🌍 Geographical Performance
- **52.57% of total revenue** comes from 5 key states, including:  
  Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana and Tamil Nadu.

### 🛒 Sales Channels
- **Amazon**: 35.51%  
- **Myntra**: 23.34%  
- **Flipkart**: 21.60%  

These platforms are the primary drivers of revenue.

### 🏷 Product Performance
- The **“Set” category contributes 49.62% of total sales**, indicating strong customer preference for bundled products  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- **Target high-value customers:**  
  Allocate a larger share of marketing budget toward **women aged 30–49**, especially on high-performing platforms  

- **Optimize channel strategy:**  
  Focus on **Amazon** as the primary revenue driver while maintaining strong presence on Myntra and Flipkart  

- **Promote top-performing products:**  
  Increase visibility and promotions for the **“Set” category** to maximize revenue  

- **Geographic expansion:**  
  Strengthen marketing efforts in top-performing states while exploring growth opportunities in underperforming regions  

---

## 💼 Business Impact
Implementing these insights can:
- Increase revenue through targeted marketing campaigns  
- Improve return on investment (ROI) by focusing on high-performing channels  
- Boost sales by promoting high-demand product categories  
- Enhance operational efficiency by maintaining high delivery success rates
