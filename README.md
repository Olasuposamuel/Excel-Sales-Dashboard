## Project objective
To analyze the sales performance for Online Store to uncover key business insights such as revenue trends, product performance and customer behavior to improve sales.

## Insight
- Analysis of the online store’s sales data reveals that March was the peak month, recording the highest sales revenue and order volume, indicating increased customer activity during this period.
- Women were the primary customers, contributing 64.05% of total purchase and they also accounted for 69.42% of total orders, showing higher engagement and purchase frequency.
- The 30–49 age group(adult) emerged as a key segment, contributing 34.59% of total orders.
- A strong operational performance was observed, with 92.25% of orders successfully delivered, indicating efficient logistics and fulfillment processes.
- Sales were highly concentrated, with Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana and Tamil Nadu being the top 5 states.
- Amazon(35.51%),Myntra(23.34%) and Flipkart(21.60%) led as the top three performing channels.
- The “Set” category dominated sales, contributing 49.62% of total revenue.

  ## Recommendation
- March is a high-sales month - we should analyze what’s driving this trend and capitalize on it with targeted promotions.
- Women are the dominant buyers – marketing efforts should focus on this demographic to maximize engagement, It would be beneficial to focus more on marketing strategies targeting women of the 30-49 age group
- Top 5 states account for 75.85% of sales – optimizing inventory and running localized campaigns in these regions could enhance sales.
- Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart contribute the most – strengthening engagements, and ad strategies on these platforms could drive further growth.
- The "Set" category is the best seller – expanding this category with new offerings could further capitalize on its strong sales performance.
